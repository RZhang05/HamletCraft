Categories from Hamlet

By: Raymond Zhang


Categories on this website each represent a type of relationship. If you were to complete the quiz that this website is centered around, you would recieve one of the types as your result. This website operates by having you complete a fun quiz which asks questions about a relationship (your choice) and categorizes it as a relationship in Hamlet, attempting to predict the result of your relationship. Analyzing relationships with gender as well as psychoanalytic theory reveal hidden characteristics in each that led to either their success or their destruction. These hidden characteristics and theories are explored in each relationship's respective page, providing insight into how the quiz is designed and what your result means. Please take the result with a grain of salt as perfect accuracy is impossible.
