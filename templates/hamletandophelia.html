Hamlet and Ophelia

By: Raymond Zhang


The relationship between Hamlet and Ophelia is affected by many external factors. Their parents, societal standards and extenuating cirumstances all result in the breakdown of the relationship and the tragedy which befalls them. First and foremost, Ophelia is subject to the impossible expectations of the patriarchal society in which she lives in.
